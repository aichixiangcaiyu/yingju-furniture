About Hebei Yingju Furniture Co., Ltd

A professional B2B hospitality furniture supplier focused on banquet chairs, church chairs, wedding chairs, banquet tables and project-based export service.

Company Introduction

Professional Hospitality Furniture Partner for Global B2B Buyers

Hebei Yingju Furniture Co., Ltd is a China-based manufacturer and exporter specializing in hotel furniture, banquet chairs, church chairs, wedding chairs and banquet tables. We serve importers, wholesalers, project contractors, event rental companies, churches and hospitality buyers who need reliable bulk supply, stable quality and efficient communication.

Our business focus is not retail. We build solutions for B2B projects. That means we care about commercial durability, customization, packing methods, container loading, lead-time communication and quotation accuracy based on real buyer requirements.

Why Choose Yingju

Clear Advantages for Overseas Buyers

This page is designed to explain not just who the company is, but why buyers should trust and work with Yingju.

Project-Oriented Supply

We understand that hospitality furniture buyers usually purchase for hotels, churches, event spaces or resale channels. Our website and communication approach are aligned with project logic rather than small retail orders.

Quotation with Real Context

Pricing is not treated as a random number. We evaluate quantity, style, frame material, fabric, packing method and destination so quotations are more relevant to actual procurement needs.

Stable Communication

International buyers need quick, clear and practical responses. We focus on confirming details, reducing misunderstandings and helping orders move forward efficiently.

Customization Capability

Many markets require different colors, finishes, upholstery or accessories. We support practical customization that helps buyers match their market demand and project design.

Export Packing Awareness

Packaging matters as much as the product itself. We pay attention to how chairs and tables are packed, stacked and loaded to improve freight efficiency and reduce shipping risk.

Long-Term Cooperation Mindset

Our goal is not just one inquiry. We want to become a dependable partner for repeat orders, new product discussions and long-term supply relationships.
