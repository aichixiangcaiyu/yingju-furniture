Customization Capability

Make Products Fit Your Market and Project Style

Different markets and projects require different colors, fabrics, seating comfort and packing methods. Yingju supports practical OEM/ODM customization for B2B buyers who need products adapted to their customer group or project design.

Customization is not only about appearance. For international trade, it also includes carton marks, packing methods, mixed-container planning and product communication before production.
