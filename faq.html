FAQ

Common questions from B2B buyers about quotation, customization, packing and shipping.

What information is needed for a quotation?

Product style, quantity, destination country or port, frame color, fabric requirement, packing method and target delivery time.

Can you customize frame color and fabric?

Yes. Frame colors, fabric, cushion, carton mark and some product details can be customized according to project requirements.

Do you support bulk orders and container loading?

Yes. The site is positioned for B2B bulk order and project supply. Packing and 40HQ loading can be discussed.

Can you supply mixed products?

Yes. Banquet chairs, church chairs, wedding chairs and banquet tables can be discussed as mixed-container projects.

Need a project quotation?

Send quantity, destination, style, color and packing requirement.
